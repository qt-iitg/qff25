About Qiskit Fall Fest

Celebrating Quantum Computing Innovation

Qiskit Fall Fest is a global celebration of quantum computing that brings together students, researchers, and industry professionals to explore the fascinating world of quantum technologies. This year, IIT Guwahati is proud to host this prestigious event.

Our fest features cutting-edge workshops, inspiring talks from leading experts, hands-on coding sessions, and networking opportunities that will help you dive deeper into quantum computing and its applications.
