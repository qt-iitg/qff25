About Qiskit Fall Fest

Experience the Quantum Era with Qiskit

Qiskit Fall Fest 2025 is a celebration of curiosity and discovery in the world of quantum computing. Organized with support from IBM Quantum and powered by Qiskit, IBM’s open source framework for quantum programming, it brings together students, researchers and innovators to learn, collaborate and imagine what is possible with this emerging technology. The fest will highlight one hundred years of progress in quantum science while opening doors to the future through workshops, coding sessions and inspiring talks from leading voices in the field.

IIT Guwahati is proud to host this historic edition of the fest. Whether you are taking your first steps in quantum computing or looking to deepen your skills, you will find opportunities to explore, connect and grow. Join us to experience hands on learning, meaningful discussions and a community that believes quantum technology will shape the future of science and society.
